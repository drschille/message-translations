Sermon Translation: The Deep to Deep

Now, we are thinking this morning upon the subject of the Word of God, and how that the Word is a Seed that a sower went forth to sow. And we know that the seed must fall into the right kind of ground in order to bring forth its kind.

And we are looking at this specific paragraph that has undergone several revisions to ensure the translation captures the exact spiritual nuance of the original spoken word in 1954.
